# Positioning

## Public wedge

OBEY makes AI obey inside real business workflows.

## Internal role of COS

COS is the internal proactive operating layer behind the work.

## What not to say

- "AI can do everything by itself"
- "COS is already a full autonomous employee"
- "This works with zero setup"
- "This replaces all judgment"

## What to emphasize

- command -> context -> approval -> execution
- better continuity than raw AI usage
- real workflows, not abstract prompting
- visible proof over vague claims

## Current authority angle

Frank is not selling prompts.

Frank is building and operating systems that make AI execution more reliable, more contextual, and more useful in real work.
